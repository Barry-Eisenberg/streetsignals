How momentum and rank scoring works

Momentum compares the last 45 days of related signals with the prior 45-day window. Initiative and segment rank show which themes and institution categories have the highest cumulative importance in the current filtered view. Importance now uses weekly recency buckets with durability floors for structurally meaningful signals. When you choose a "For Whom" lens, the catalogue re-ranks by weighted relevance using use-case match, keyword fit, audience fit, and freshness.

Debug key: u=use case, p=primary keyword hits, s=secondary hits, a=audience hints, r=recency weight, u+=use-case boost, i+=importance boost, x-=anti-signal penalty, score=final weighted relevance.
