Institutional Positioning Radar

Want to know where your firm stands? Benchmark your institution's digital-asset posture across tokenized funds, stablecoins & settlement, and DLT-based market infrastructure and see how you compare to peers.

Best for:

  • Boards, CIOs, and strategy leaders at asset managers, banks, custodians, and FMIs.
  • Fintech and infrastructure providers wanting a clear view of target institutions' adoption profiles.
Radar views are based on SftS signal importance tiers (Structural, Material, Context) and trend context across the last 12 months. Optional on-chain context from Dune, rwa.xyz, and other public datasets can be shown in the theme tabs but does not affect radar scores.
Methodology

How the radar is scored

Each institution in the SftS catalogue is scored across the three themes using cumulative signal importance, recency weighting, and stage mix (Structural vs Material vs Exploratory) across the last 12 months. Peer group averages are computed across all institutions in that category.

Signal breadth

Number of distinct signals across each theme — shows commitment and activity level relative to peers.

Importance depth

Cumulative importance-weighted score per theme — prioritizes Structural and high-materiality signals over noise.

Recency

Recent signals (last 90 days) carry higher weight — distinguishes active institutions from legacy positioners.
